Support and Optimization options

Implement Sales Cloud in your business

Sales Cloud is the Salesforce solution designed to improve sales teams performance.

At Stellaxius, we believe that each implementation is different, as it can be based on standard features or more customized. The most suitable for your business is dependent on your current situation, needs, wants, and goals, as well as, on the complexity of your business processes. Rely on us to help you pick what best fits you!

Standard Implementation

Every implementation at Stellaxius starts with an evaluation of your company’s current situation, needs and goals, through which our consultants go through your business processes to understand the requirements to create a valid technological solution.

In the standard implementation, the solution will be designed using Salesforce’s out of the box features as the basis to map business requirements into Sales Cloud. A solution based on Salesforce’s standard features is possible when the company doesn’t have lots of complexity in its processes, coming from other tools integration for example.

Customized Implementation

When companies are running their business across an application map that is complex, the best way to integrate and harmonize business processes and tools is through a customized implementation, tailor-made to fit your needs.

In a customized implementation, it is possible to use Salesforce’s standard features coupled with tailor-made features and integrations. Our consultants will capture your business needs and processes, in order to build a data-model and system architecture that can support all your business processes.

Some common tools our customers have chosen to integrate based on their business requirements are CTI tools, ERP’s, CPQ, marketing automation, gamification tools, among others. Just tell us about your needs and we will recommend the solution we find most suitable, in accordance with Salesforce best practices.

Need Sales Cloud Support?

Whether you already know what your Sales Cloud needs or you are looking for help figuring it out, we got you!

Stellaxius relies on dedicated teams available to book a meeting and get an overview on the key points that you would like to boost in your current Sales Cloud implementation. We offer a variety of support options to guide you through the diagnosis and design of solutions that can help you achieve your goals.

Assessment

If you feel your Salesforce is underperforming or want to improve it even further, our consultants can be your new set of eyes. By performing a Sales Cloud Assessment, we will go through your org and build a thorough report about its current state and give actionable recommendations to take your Salesforce to the next level.

System Enhancement

Our consultants can help you creating new features, custom reports, automations or even lightning components to introduce additional custom development to improve and bring clarity on the way you see your data.

Application Maintenance Services

If what you need is just an hour package to solve small issues this solution is for you. Our maintenance packages can give support to your users in their admin tasks, help troubleshooting and correcting bugs, and help preventing future problems with system evolution maintenance.

Lightning Migration

If you have a Salesforce Classic Org and want to switch to Lightning, in order to increase revenue, reduce costs or just to take advantage of the Lightning exclusive 850+ features, we can support your Admin Team in the transition process.

Integrations

We help you connect Salesforce with other apps and existing business systems, facilitating data migration and synchronization between different systems.

Adoption Management

Sometimes, the most difficult part when implementing a new technology is adoption. Our team is here to help you understand how to improve the adoption levels in your company.
