Implement Sales Cloud in your business

Sales Cloud is the Salesforce solution designed to improve sales teams performance.

At Stellaxius, we believe that each implementation is different, as it can be based on standard features or more customized. The most suitable for your business is dependent on your current situation, needs, wants, and goals, as well as, on the complexity of your business processes. Rely on us to help you pick what best fits you!

Standard Implementation

Every implementation at Stellaxius starts with an evaluation of your company’s current situation, needs and goals, through which our consultants go through your business processes to understand the requirements to create a valid technological solution.

In the standard implementation, the solution will be designed using Salesforce’s out of the box features as the basis to map business requirements into Sales Cloud. A solution based on Salesforce’s standard features is possible when the company doesn’t have lots of complexity in its processes, coming from other tools integration for example.

Customized Implementation

When companies are running their business across an application map that is complex, the best way to integrate and harmonize business processes and tools is through a customized implementation, tailor-made to fit your needs.

In a customized implementation, it is possible to use Salesforce’s standard features coupled with tailor-made features and integrations. Our consultants will capture your business needs and processes, in order to build a data-model and system architecture that can support all your business processes.

Some common tools our customers have chosen to integrate based on their business requirements are CTI tools, ERP’s, CPQ, marketing automation, gamification tools, among others. Just tell us about your needs and we will recommend the solution we find most suitable, in accordance with Salesforce best practices.

Pardot Assessment

What does it mean to do an Assessment?

Our consultants will go through your Pardot account to technically and functionally audit it, in order to understand if you have issues that need to be solved or if you have underused functionalities that could be of use to your business, for example. Besides auditing the system, our team will shadow your team to get a sense of how they use the system, so that we can help your team getting the most value from Pardot in their daily activities. By partnering with your team we will also be able to understand your company processes and validate if there’s match between processes and technology or if there’s some work that can be done to increase the match between the company processes, business needs and technology.
