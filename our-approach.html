The Discovery Process

Get to know you:

Let’s get to know you! We want to truly understand your vision, needs, pain points, and the biggest opportunities you have ahead! So, just like suggested in the golden circle, we firstly start with the “why”. Why are you looking for business innovation with Salesforce solutions? Tells us about your purpose, vision, and goals!

Understand your business context:

After finding where you want to go, let’s focus on where you are at! Let us discover where we are starting from. For that, we will gather and clarify requirements received from multiple channels. Our Salesforce certified project managers will map your current digital process and learn about your methodologies to identify gaps. In the end, we will have a blueprint to guide us throughout the process. Learn how we handle business analysis.

The Solution Path

After conducting our business analysis, we design, develop, and implement digital solutions that best suit your business.

Plan & Design

We plan the implementation with your teams, looking at both business and IT constraints and opportunities, leveraging design patterns successfully used with other customers and applying the appropriate governance. In the end we will have a blueprint to guide us throughout the process.

Test & Release

We validate the solution, offering you the opportunity to review it as we prepare the different work streams to come together in a release. When third-party systems are involved, we also perform integration testing and confirm that the interfaces are ready for delivery.

Code & Build

We configure and code your solution, following a set of defined development standards that we keep improving on through our academy and delivery factory. We ensure the use of code repositories and follow a strategy to continuously build a release of requested features.

Deploy, Operate & Monitor

We complete the process of delivering the features you requested through a deployment from a sandbox to your production environment. We work with your operations teams to ensure coordination, ownership of the deployment process and monitoring for increased quality.

The solution process is not linear. On the contrary. At Stellaxius we use Agile, the approach to software development that inputs flexibility and continuous evolution of the project requirements. So, as the project develops, our Salesforce solution may be enhanced and adapted to improve results.

Agile flexible solutions allow us to:

  • Best focus on your business
  • Reduce unnecessary bureaucracy
  • Decrease your investment’s risk
  • Respond faster to change

Agile flexible solutions need to be cross-functional and cross-roles. And, we cannot forget that organisations are made of people. Our agile coaches have the knowledge and techniques to teach and engage everyone in the organisation, enabling them to understand how they can make a difference. We help driving transformation across departments, engaging your executive and management teams, aligning IT and business analysts, and getting the organisation in the same boat to change incrementally. At the end of the day, your organization will be working in synergy.

Learn more about our software development expertise here.

Final Destination

Arrive Safe and Sound:

The final destination of our projects goes beyond “getting the job done”. The implemented digital tools are only ready for use and the project is only successfully completed when it:

  • Finishes on time
  • Within budget
  • Reaches the desired destination

Fly the nest with Salesforce training:

Change management is key to assure the solution is delivered beyond your expectations and gets successfully adopted. So, rely on our Salesforce certified project managers to coach, train, and support your teams, applying collaboration techniques to ensure they are the ones driving the change, as we remain as invisible as possible. Our mission is for you to become as autonomous and independent as possible when it comes to use and manage your implemented digital solutions.

We’ll keep an eye on you...

When Salesforce process management is done correctly, it brings great power to an organization. And, with great power comes great responsibility. So, we will not leave you on duty all by yourself. Assuring the expected results and track benefits realization is fundamental. So, we put processes and tools in place to define, measure, and monitor the KPIs that everyone needs, so that they know if they are on track or need adjustment.

Learn more about Program and Process Management here.
