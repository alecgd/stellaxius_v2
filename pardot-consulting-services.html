Standard Implementation

Is this approach for you?

Standard Implementation is for those to whom the only piece missing is marketing automation! What do we mean by this? Marketers who are familiar with marketing automation and are already using digital channels to put in practice their integrated marketing strategy.

How do we help?

Our consultants will team up with you to understand the goals you have for your marketing automation implementation and for your marketing strategy to gather business requirements that have to be translated into Pardot. We will use standard Pardot functionalities to get you all set and running with the solution as quick as possible.

Our goal is to deliver the solution you are expecting as fast as possible, respecting budget and timing without compromising quality, because that’s what we really offer.
