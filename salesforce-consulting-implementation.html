Our Marketing Automation Solutions

Our Service and Customer Support Solutions

Implementing Salesforce Service solutions in your business, helps your team to anticipate your customers’ needs and address them faster and more accurately. Thus, you will be delivering nothing but the ultimate customer experience!

Salesforce Service Cloud

Salesforce Service Cloud helps you improve customer satisfaction, by giving agents a deeper understanding of customers and boost efficiency by making it easier to follow support cases. We configure your Service Cloud so you can best measure performance with analytics, and reduce case volume with a branded self-service portal with peer-shared knowledge.

CTI for Salesforce

A CTI system allows you to integrate your phone with your CRM. The integration helps sales reps to personalize their calls. It also allows service agents to reduce call handle times and increase first call resolution rates, by providing the information they need to promptly solve cases. CTI solutions integrate with both Service and Sales Cloud, enabling better support for customers and potential ones.
